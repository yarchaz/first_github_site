Discription

All people living in or visiting Preston, Soda Spring, or Fish Haven areas. These will be people who have jobs in, activities around, or trips to these areas. They will be people who have access to the internet whether on their home computer or their hand devices. Anyone looking to plan out their day according to the weather will be the primary focus of our site.

Personas

Jeff Green

  • Job: construction
  • Age: 27
  • Education: Some College
  • Family Status: Married with a little girl.

Jeff looks at the weather everyday to know if he needs to bring his rain jacket with him to work. Knowing the weather also helps him know what equipment to use on a job as some tools can become unsafe to handle. Jeff also likes to plan weekend outings with his wife and daughter, so he really enjoys having a 10 day forecast to reference. He expects the information he needs to be easily accessible and viewable on any device.

Scenarios

  • What's the weather like right now?

    • People getting ready for the day and want to know what to wear.

  • What will the weather be like tomorrow/next week?

    • people tying to plan an activity in advance. seeking to know if a particualre day will have favorable conditions for it.

  • Is it a good idea to___?

    • people who have an activity in mind but are unsure if the weather will permit it.

  • What's the chance of rain today?

    • People wanting to know if they should bring an umbrella to the park

  • How long is this storm going to last?

    • People who have been stuck indoors all day and want to know when they can go outside.
