Section 1

The Albuquerque New Mexico Temple is the 73ed operating temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Later-day Saints and was dedicated on March 5th, 2000 making it the first temple built in New Mexico.

An oasis in the desert, the Albuquerque temple grounds are filled with green grass and a variety of trees, flowers, and shrubs. A majestic fountain is placed at the entrance and brings joy to patrons entering and leaving the temple. The exterior of the temple is covered in heavenly symbols including suns, moons, and stars, and the Angel Moroni tops the temple spire.
